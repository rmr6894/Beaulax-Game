1- Flashlight State (Boolean): Wether player has flashlight or not
2- Jumppack State (Boolean): Wether player has Jumppack or not
3- Spacesuit State (Boolean): Wether player has Spacesuit or not
4- Access Level State (Int): Gives the player's Access Level, from 0-4
5- Location X (Int): Gives player's location in x-axis
6- Location Y (Int): Gives player's location in y-axis
